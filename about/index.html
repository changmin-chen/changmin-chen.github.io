Biography

I am Chang-Min Chen, a recent master's graduate in medical device and imaging, with extensive experience in medical data research and programming.

My focus areas include signal processing, image analysis, and statistical analysis, and I have previously been involved in image algorithm development at a technology company.

In addition to my technical abilities, my clinical internship experience has molded me into an outstanding team collaborator. I excel at communicating and working closely with colleagues, with a deep understanding of the requirements of medical imaging examinations.

Furthermore, I bring international experience to the table, having interned at a hospital in Japan and presented my research findings in English at an international conference in Canada.
